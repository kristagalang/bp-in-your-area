About BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. The group debuted on August 8, 2016, with their album Square One. In just a short amount of time, BLACKPINK has already managed to set many records, some of which are listed below.

  • The most followed girl group on Spotify.
  • The most subscribed Asian female act on YouTube.
  • The first K-pop girl group to perform at Coachella.
  • The first Korean act to have three music videos with over one billion views on YouTube.
  • Their music videos for "Kill This Love" and "How You Like That" both set records for the most-viewed music video within 24 hours of release.
